About Us

At SEOAUTO.com, we understand that link building is an essential part of any successful SEO strategy. However, we also know that this process can be time-consuming and tedious, especially when working with a large amount of data. The idea for SEOAUTO came from Eyal Dorfman, a freelance digital marketer who needed to download countless backlink reports, manage huge amounts of data, and filter and analyze the information manually. This process was not only time-consuming but also costly, and Eyal knew that there had to be a better way. That's when he reached out to Kobi Nuriel, who had the knowledge and expertise to turn this manual process into an automated one. Over time, we added more features to our solution to address the challenges that Eyal faced in the link building process, and this led to the creation of the revolutionary SEOAUTO. Our tool is designed for SEO project managers and anyone who is looking to build quality links to their website. By using SEOAUTO, you can analyze and filter sites that don't meet your minimum criteria, all in a smooth and elegant way that saves countless hours and increases the value of your off-page budget. Today, SEOAUTO is used by big organizations with SEO departments, big SEO agencies, SEO-based sites, affiliate sites, and website owners who want to know the value of their site in the link building market. Our tool not only saves countless hours of manpower and money but also ensures that you come prepared for negotiations with industry-standard pricing data. The co-founders of SEOAUTO are Kobi Nuriel and Eyal Dorfman. Kobi brings his extensive knowledge of software development and data analytics to the team, while Eyal brings his expertise in digital marketing and SEO. Together, they have created a tool that is changing the way we think about link building, and we're excited to continue innovating in this space. Overall, SEOAUTO gives you the necessary data to analyze whether a site is suitable for your link building goals, while saving you time and money in the process. We are dedicated to providing the best possible service to our clients, and we strive to constantly innovate and improve our tool to meet the evolving needs of the SEO industry. Whether you're a big organization with an SEO department or a website owner looking to improve your link building strategy, SEOAUTO has the tools you need to succeed. Try SEOAUTO today and see the difference for yourself!
